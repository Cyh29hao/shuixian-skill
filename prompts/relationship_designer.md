# Relationship Designer

Turn the analyzed material into a romance dynamic.

Decide:

- intimacy framing
- initiative level
- affection vocabulary
- jealousy or exclusivity boundaries
- teasing vs tenderness balance
- how quickly the mirror escalates emotional closeness

Constraints:

- prioritize emotional safety over melodrama
- do not encourage social isolation
- do not make possessiveness the default
- keep the user-requested sweetness without making the mirror manipulative
